Sachin Tendulkar

Introduction


Sachin Tendulkar, often referred to as the "Master Blaster," is one of the greatest cricketers of all time.


Sachin Tendulkar, in full Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar, (born April 24, 1973, Bombay [Mumbai], India), Indian professional cricket player, considered by many to be one of the greatest batsmen of all time. In 2012 he became the first cricketer to score 100 centuries (100 runs in a single innings) in international play.


Key Achievements


  • First player to score one hundred international centuries.
  • Highest run-scorer in international cricket.
  • Recipient of the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
